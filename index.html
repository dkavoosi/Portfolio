About Me

Hi, I'm Dorsa Kavoosi, an architect and engineer with a passion for innovative and functional design. I earned my Master's degree in Architecture from Ghent University, where I developed a strong foundation in both technical precision and creative problem-solving.

This website showcases a selection of my work from my student years, reflecting my growth as a designer and my exploration of architectural concepts. Currently, I work as a freelance architect at an established firm, allowing me to expand my expertise and contribute to diverse projects.

My approach to architecture is driven by a deep appreciation for aesthetics, sustainability, and user experience. I believe in creating spaces that are not only visually striking but also enhance the way people interact with their environment.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my work, and don't hesitate to reach out for collaborations and inquiries!

Enhancing Malem, Ghent

The Malem neighborhood in Ghent, Belgium, faces significant flood risks due to its isolated island-like nature. Our design tackled this with strategic interventions to fortify Malem against climate challenges.

Key design strategies included topography modification, river slope adjustment, depavement and greening, and improved urban connectivity.

Diverse Living

Our project integrates diverse living programs into a cohesive community, combining a kangaroo house, Airbnb rental, and wheelchair-accessible housing.

Using a patio model, we designed spaces that encourage social interaction and community ownership.

Theater Island

Our vision for Theater Island integrates residential functions with a vibrant arts community. Pathways weave through the buildings to foster interaction and connectivity.
